App Concept

We have chosen Use Case C (Your Own).

The Problem: Magic: The Gathering players often struggle to digitize their physical collections, check card prices during trades, or look up rules quickly during gameplay. Manual entry is slow and prone to error.

The Solution: A native Android app that leverages computer vision (Camera) to instantly identify physical cards. It provides an immediate overlay with card data and allows users to add the card to a local "Deck Builder" inventory with a single tap.

Target User:

  • Competitive Players: Who need to digitize decklists for tournament registration.
  • Collectors: Who want to track the value and quantity of their owned cards.
  • Casual Players: Who need quick rule lookups during games.

Similar Applications:

TCGPlayer, Delver Lens, Dragon Shield Scanner
